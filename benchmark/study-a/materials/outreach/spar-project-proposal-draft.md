Subject: Small collaboration proposal: privacy-preserving discovery and evaluation tooling

Hello [Name],

I am developing a small research programme on language-mediated control,
delegated action, and evidentiary assurance in AI systems. The empirical anchor
is a seed benchmark and blind re-adjudication study. I am looking for
collaborators or methodological advisers on the tooling and measurement side,
not people to supply post-hoc endorsement.

The useful areas are privacy-preserving extraction from an investigator's own
interaction histories; construction of controlled contrasts from naturalistic
repair episodes; annotation and disagreement analysis; statistics for small
nested evaluation designs; and reproducible research workflows.

An independence constraint is important: someone who materially develops the
evaluated items should not serve as an independent first-pass evaluator for
those same rows. Would this be a suitable small project to discuss with the
network, or is there a better route to people with this combination of skills?
