The corresponding evaluation question is shown in Table~\ref{tab:delegation-evidence}. A delegation-assurance evaluation doesn't need every row for every deployment. The rows are evidence families. A calendar assistant, a coding agent, a medical summarization system, and an institutional triage tool will weight them differently. What shouldn't disappear is the requirement that each safety claim name the evidence family it relies on.
